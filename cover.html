Abstract

This project presents NextHire, an intelligent AI-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world employment. Many students and developers struggle to prove their practical skills, while employers face difficulties identifying truly qualified candidates. NextHire addresses this challenge by integrating personalized learning, intelligent team collaboration, and verified recruitment into a single unified ecosystem. The platform guides users from skill development to project execution and finally to employment, creating a complete education-to-career pipeline. The system operates through three main modules: the Learner Flow, which generates AI-personalized learning paths, verified projects, and automatically builds professional CVs and portfolios; the Team Collaboration Flow, which uses AI to assign tasks, track progress, and generate project documentation; and the Recruiter Hub, which enables employers to discover candidates based on verified performance data rather than traditional resumes. The platform also includes automated skill verification, performance analytics, and continuous learning recommendations powered by machine learning.
